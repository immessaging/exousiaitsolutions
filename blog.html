🎉 Launching Our New Dashboard

Posted on July 8, 2025 by Admin

We’re thrilled to unveil our brand new student dashboard—a personalized space where coders can spin challenges, track progress, and earn badges.

The dashboard includes a spinner for creative tasks, video tutorials, badge management, and certificate generation. This gamified approach boosts student motivation and gives teachers better tools to monitor growth. Parents can now view progress with clean visuals and upcoming milestones—so everyone stays in the loop.
