🎨 Creative Coding Adventure

Welcome to a joyful launchpad for young creators! This course introduces students to visual coding using Scratch, MIT App Inventor, and other playful programming platforms. With drag-and-drop blocks and animated logic, kids build games, stories, and apps that reflect their imaginations.

Every module is packed with discovery, creativity, and confidence-building tasks. Whether animating their favorite pet, designing quiz apps for family, or building mini games that respond to clicks, this experience turns code into a canvas!

🧭 Learning Quest

Each student follows their own hero journey in coding:

  • Stage 1: Explore — Meet characters, environments, and buttons!
  • Stage 2: Build — Make things move, respond, and win points.
  • Stage 3: Customize — Add sound, themes, and personal art.
  • Stage 4: Share — Publish or present to friends and family!

🎮 Themed Project Kits

Students select their favorite kits to kickstart learning:

  • 🦸 Superhero Game: Jump, dodge, save the day!
  • 🐱 Pet Story Creator: Animate a cat's daily routine.
  • 💡 Invention Simulator: Use App Inventor to build an idea tester!
  • 🎤 Music Mixer: Trigger notes and dance moves with Scratch.

🧪 Surprise Challenge Spinner

Feeling brave? Hit the button to reveal your next coding mission!

📝 Personalized Story Task

Choose a favorite character (animal, hero, object), invent a quest, then animate it in Scratch:

  • Create 3 scenes with movement and sound.
  • Use events like when clicked or when space key pressed.
  • End with a custom message for viewers!

Kids won’t just learn—they’ll laugh, imagine, problem-solve, and grow. Parents, brace yourselves: your children might soon be teaching you how to code! 😄
